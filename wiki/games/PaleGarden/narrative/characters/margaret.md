# Margaret — Character Arc

Elderly neighbor. Lives on the next farm. Widowed. Keeps chickens.
Bakes bread when worried. Comes over more than strictly necessary.

## Personality
Warm. Practical. Quietly perceptive. Does not say what she suspects.
Has seen enough of the world to recognize wrongness without naming it.

## Act 1 role
Fence repair sidequest (Day 10-11). Bread deliveries after.
Quietly monitors Elias. Looks at the north corner without commenting.

## Trust system
Trust -5 to +5. Starts at 0.
High trust (≥3): she confides she has been asking around about the market man.
Low trust (≤-2): she still comes. She just does not stay for tea.

## Act 4 (Day 42): Always visits. Her fate depends on every choice before this point.
