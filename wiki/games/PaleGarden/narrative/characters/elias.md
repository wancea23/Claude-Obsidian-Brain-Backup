# Elias — Character Arc

Elias is the player character. Young farmer, recently inherited the farm.
Quiet. Self-reliant. Prone to rationalising strange things away.

## Act 1 (Days 1–14): The Good Life
Settles in. Finds rhythm in farm work. Cautiously lets Bruno in.
Avoids the north corner without quite knowing why.
Buys the seed on Day 14. Tells himself it is a business decision.

## Act 2 (Days 15–25): The Awakening
Hears the plant for the first time. Chooses to interpret it as stress.
His journal entries grow shorter. The rationalising gets thinner.
