# The Seller — Character Arc

An old man. He is always at the far edge of whatever market he appears at.
He does not call out. He is perfectly still.

## What is known
He has been at markets in this region since at least 1962 (Margaret has a photograph).
He only ever has one item for sale.
His eyes, examined closely, are not human.

## Dialogue philosophy
Maximum 6 words per line. Never answers directly. Never repeats himself.
He already knows what the player will choose.

## Act 1 role
Visible but unreachable on Day 7. Fully accessible Day 14.
The JS-01 jumpscare triggers on second examination.

## Broader arc
He is a recurring figure across games in this world. He is patient.
He does not recruit. He offers. The choosing is always the player's.
